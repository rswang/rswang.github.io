---
title: Banana Bread
image: /images/recipes/banana-bread.jpg
date: 2026-06-27
blurb: A moist loaf for very ripe bananas
ingredients:
  - 113g unsalted butter, softened
  - 160g granulated sugar
  - 2 large eggs
  - 4 very ripe bananas, mashed
  - 187g flour
  - 1 tsp baking soda
  - ½ tsp salt
  - ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
steps:
  - Heat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease and flour a 9x5-inch (23x13cm) loaf pan.
  - Cream the butter and sugar until light. Beat in the eggs, then the crushed bananas.
  - In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir into the banana mixture along with the vanilla, just until combined — don't overmix.
  - Pour into the pan and bake for 70 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool before slicing.
