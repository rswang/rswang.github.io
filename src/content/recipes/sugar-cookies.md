title: Sugar Cookies
image: /images/recipes/sugar-cookies.jpg
date: 2026-06-27
blurb: Soft cut-out cookies with a smooth, glossy icing
ingredients:
  - — For the cookies —
  - 2 sticks unsalted butter, softened
  - 175g confectioners' sugar
  - 1 egg, room temperature
  - 1½–3 tsp extract or emulsion (any flavor)
  - 1 tsp salt
  - 2 tsp baking powder
  - 400–425g all-purpose flour
  - — For the icing —
  - 375g powdered sugar, sifted
  - 2 tbsp light corn syrup
  - 3–4 tbsp milk (any kind)
  - ½ tsp vanilla extract (clear for white icing)
  - Gel food coloring (optional)
  - Sprinkles for decorating (optional)
steps:
  - Heat oven to 400°F and line baking sheets with parchment.
  - Cream the butter and confectioners' sugar. Mix in the egg and extract.
  - Sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt, and add to the wet mixture a little at a time until incorporated.
  - Roll out the dough and cut into shapes, dusting with flour as needed. Bake 7–8 minutes, until the tops look dry rather than shiny — don't let them brown.
  - Cool the cookies completely before icing.
  - "Make the icing: whisk together the powdered sugar, corn syrup, vanilla, and 2 tbsp milk. Add more milk a teaspoon at a time until the icing drizzles smoothly from a whisk and holds its shape for a few seconds before settling. Tint with gel food coloring as desired."
  - Decorate the cooled cookies and add sprinkles, then let the icing set for several hours or overnight before stacking.
