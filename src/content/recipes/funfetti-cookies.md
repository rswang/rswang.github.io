---
title: Funfetti Cookies
image: /images/recipes/funfetti-cookies.jpg
date: 2026-06-27
blurb: Cake-batter chocolate chip cookies loaded with sprinkles
ingredients:
  - 167g all-purpose flour
  - 190g vanilla, yellow, or white boxed cake mix (dry mix only)
  - ½ tsp baking soda
  - ½ tsp salt
  - 1½ sticks unsalted butter, softened
  - 100g granulated sugar
  - 100g packed light brown sugar
  - 1 egg, room temperature
  - 1½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  - 180g semisweet chocolate chips
  - 80g rainbow sprinkles
steps:
  - Preheat oven to 350°F and line baking sheets with parchment.
  - Sift together the flour, cake mix, baking soda, and salt.
  - Beat the butter with both sugars on medium-high until fluffy, about 3 minutes. Mix in the egg and vanilla. Add the dry ingredients on low until just combined, then fold in the chocolate chips and sprinkles.
  - Scoop the dough with a #20 cookie scoop onto the sheets, spacing the mounds apart. Bake 13–15 minutes, until the edges are lightly browned and the centers still soft.
  - Cool on the sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
